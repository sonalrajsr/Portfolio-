    SFD & BMD plotting website

    In our second-year Strength of Materials course, a collaborative effort among three team members resulted in the creation of a dynamic website. Dedicated to the analysis of material strength, our project focused on plotting Bending Moment and Shear Force diagrams. Through a harmonious blend of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the Chart.js library, our website provided a comprehensive tool for structural analysis. This collaborative endeavor not only demonstrated our technical proficiency but also underscored the synergy achieved through teamwork in tackling complex engineering challenges.
